Iwona DeSouza

I'm a Senior Technical Writer at Boomi, where I own documentation for AI Agents, GraphQL APIs, and the Agent Control Tower — translating intricate backend architecture, including Snowflake integrations and OpenAPI specs, into guidance developers can actually act on.

Beyond writing individual docs, I care about how documentation is structured and discovered. I've led full information architecture overhauls to break down content silos, produced technical videos to complement written docs, and act as the day-to-day liaison between Product and Engineering to keep documentation aligned with rapid release cycles.

My path here wasn't linear. I studied History, then Advertising & Corporate Communications, having worked in multi-disciplinary environments that ranged from a historical travelogue on Egypt to a Scopus-indexed research paper titled "How offensive is offensive; A closer look into Controversial advertising" which I had the honour of presenting at a conference in Belgium. That grounding in storytelling and structured thinking still shapes how I approach technical writing: start from what the reader needs to understand, then build the structure that gets them there.

I'm currently expanding into UX writing, AI documentation strategy, and developer content systems — the layer above individual docs, where structure, product messaging, and writing meet.
